LinkedIn uses OAuth 2.0 for authentication and communication. In order for users to authenticate with the LinkedIn API, you must specify the ID, secret and redirect URI of your LinkedIn app. You can create a new app at the following URL: https://www.linkedin.com/developer/apps

Notice: When a user authenticates with the LinkedIn API, an access token is saved along with information about the user. With the LinkedIn API, access tokens will expire after roughly two months.
